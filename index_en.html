    Are there any competitors?

    In 2018, VR technology is recognized as the technology of the year. Many market leaders have already announced innovative implementations in their business. For example, from 2020 Mercedes launches a virtual showroom with a full demonstration of their cars.
    Amazon announced to its investors that from 2020 a virtual online store will launch. There are many companies develop games and entertainment content. And for business industry, VR / AR tools and software are very limited.
    ShowMeBiz develops a platform like constructor to help any business to digitize itself for virtual reality in a short time and with a minimum budget. In addition, the business model is created in such a way where all project participants win: investors, partners, tenant companies, users. Thus, we can safely say that ShowMeBiz has no direct competitors.

    What is the difference between the G&V expo platform and other exhibition applications?

    For event organizers there are so many different solutions. And individually developed applications, and universal applications already exist. The main difference between such solutions and the G&V expo platform is that the G&Vexpo is a platform with VR/AR functionality.
    The goal of the platform is to attract more target audience traffic for exhibiting companies. With this functionality, companies are interested in this high level of exhibitions, and the organizers have the opportunity to attract more participants.
    And accordingly, the organizers have the opportunity to digitize their exhibition and monetize it during long time, selling paid content and virtual stands of their exhibitors.

    What makes the ExpoCoin (EXPO) token unique?

    The Token ExpoCoin (EXPO) was created for a traditional business with the goal of keeping all transactions inside the G&V expo platform. Thus, the token value is determined by the reinforcement of each unit with live fiat money in the system.

    What is the minimum tokens amount to buy?

    The minimum tokens amount is 100 ExpoCoin. The price per each is 1,50$

    If the project does not go, is it possible to return the investment?

    ShowMeBiz is raising funds for the G&Vexpo project. Funds invested by investors do not return! The company works on renting ExpoBlock to share passive income with its investors. The process has already been launched, the platform is technically implemented for a full start and monetization. Investors with their investments support the project. G&Vexpo is not an investment company, but an IT product. By investing the investor has the right to claim a profit from the lease of its assets.

    Will profit depend on rate of ExpoCoin?

    At the moment, the rate of ExpoCoin is $1.50. For a tenant company, the cost of the token is $ 1.75. The price is fixed.

    When and how can I withdraw my ExpoCoin to fiat money (dollars, euros, etc.)?

    It is planned to open an internal exchange market and exchanger where participants will be able to exchange their tokens at a recommended price of $1.75. A token is not being withdrawn to an external token sale market in order to avoid market manipulations. According to forecasts, the price of the token will grow, which will have a positive effect on investors' profits.

    When will my frozen blocks become active and start making profit?

    Silver ExpoBlock have 40% of rental income. When buying tokens, assets are frozen for 3 months. After 3 months from the date of purchase, ExpoBlock starts to be leased and, accordingly, participates in the profits distribution. Also ExpoCoin will be available for withdrawal on the internal exchange market and exchanger.
